About Our Farm

For over 15 years, we've been raising livestock the way it should be done - with plenty of space, good feed, and patience. Our cattle and sheep graze on open pastures here in Egypt,
where we can keep an eye on every animal and ensure they're healthy and well-cared for.

We started this farm because we believe families deserve to know where their meat comes from. When you order from us, you're getting livestock raised naturally, without shortcuts.
Whether you need a whole animal or select cuts, we handle everything from raising to delivery.

What We Offer

We specialize in grass-fed cattle and pasture-raised sheep. Each animal is raised on our farm from birth, so we can guarantee the quality. You can browse available livestock, place your order
online, and schedule delivery or farm pickup at your convenience.

Why Choose Us

Quality doesn't happen by accident. We take the time to do things right - from rotational grazing that keeps our pastures healthy, to working with local butchers who understand proper cuts.
When you order from us, you're supporting a local farm and getting meat you can trust.

Have questions? Feel free to reach out - we're always happy to talk about our animals and how we raise them.
